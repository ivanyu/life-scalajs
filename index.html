Information

You can find more information about the game in Wikipedia.

The world in this implementation is toroidal - top and bottom, left and right edges are connected.

During the simulation, the history of the university evolution is recorded, and you can scroll to previous states when the simulation is stopped. Also when the simulation is stopped you can change the state by adding or removing living cells. However, the history will be cleared after this changes.

You can control the speed of the simulation and sound volume and zoom the cell plane by moving the sliders at the top.
